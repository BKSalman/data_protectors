Data Protectors

Cyber Organizational Design

Product Overview

A comprehensive strategic approach to orchestrate the transformation of Cybersecurity Programs towards more secure, reliable, & efficient centers.


KSA High Demands

Organizations in Saudi Arabia are rapidly moving toward evolved digital transformations to align with the Kingdom's 2030 vision. As this new shift emerged, there is now a significant need for establishing and developing advanced cybersecurity programs to achieve businesses objectives while ensuring their information systems and data are protected.


Product Objectives

To address the never-ending demands for technology adoptions while ensuring safeguards controls are in place, an organization must establish/maintain an effective Cybersecurity Organizational Design.

  • The Challenge
  • The Solution
  • Key Objectives

The Challenge

A large organization ranked among the top Fortune 500 corporations in United States suffered from a major Cybersecurity incident. As a leading provider of FinTech solutions and commercial banking services, the organization applied advanced cyber controls and safeguards. However, the incident occurred and a thorough investigation was conducted. The comprehensive set of root cause analysis, assessments, and post incident findings have identified a need to enhance the Cybersecurity Organizational design as well as roles and responsibilities related to Cybersecurity across the enterprise.

The Solution

As part of the action plan to address the gaps identified, one of Data Protectors founders led a designated workstream to orchestrate the transformation of the Cyber Organizational structure.

Key Objectives

Evaluate the current Cyber organizational structure and stature of the CISO responsibilities including authority, ownership, and accountability in relevant governance forums.

Develop and implement enhancements for the Cybersecurity Organizational design.
